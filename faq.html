FAQ

Why is the sky blue?

Because blue light is scattered more in the sky by having a shorter wavelength than the other colors

Why grass green?

Grass and other plants use a chemical called chlorophyll to get energy from the sun. chlorophyll is green.

Why is blood red?

Blood is red because of hemoglobin in the blood cells used for carrying oxygen. The red comes from the iron in the hemoglobin molecule.
